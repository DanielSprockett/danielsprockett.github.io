Welcome!


I am a PhD candidate in David Relman's Lab at the Stanford School of Medicine, where I study host-associated microbial communities. My research is centered on identifying where your microbiome comes from in early life, as well as the complex forces that shape it as it develops into a stable and resilient ecological system.

Click around to learn more about my research interests, read some of my publications, or use one of the R packages that I've developed.




Research Interests

  • Host-Microbiome Interactions
  • Ecological Theory
  • Genome Evolution
  • Scientific Reproducibility
